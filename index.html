Instructions

  • Click to enter first-person mode
  • WASD or Arrow keys to move
  • Shift to move up, Space to move down
  • Mouse to look around
  • Left click to place cubes
  • Right click to remove cubes
  • Scroll wheel to cycle colors
  • Cubes stack on top of each other
  • ESC to exit first-person mode
  • Select a color from the palette
